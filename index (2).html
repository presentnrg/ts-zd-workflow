üë§ Human Touchpoints Summary
Every stage of the support experience has a clear path to a real person.
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî First Contact

AI Greeting Mentions Humans

The very first message the customer sees says: "if I'm not able to quickly resolve your issue, I can share your message with our human support staff."
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî Every Email

Email Responses Invite Reply

Every AI-generated email explicitly says: "If we haven't resolved your concern, please reply, and a member of the Time Study Tech Support team will respond."
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî Agent Notification

Tier 1 Agents See Every Ticket (Email & Async Channels)

A trigger notifies all Tier 1 agents whenever a new ticket is created via email or async channels ‚Äî nothing goes unreviewed, even if the AI resolved it. Note: Generative AI messaging tickets are manually checked daily rather than generating a notification email.
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî Messaging Fallback

Fallback ‚Üí "Provide Your Contact Info"

When the AI can't answer in messaging, it presents a button to leave contact information for a human to follow up directly.
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî Messaging On-Demand

AI Offers Human Help Once Per Conversation

In every messaging conversation, the AI mentions once that the customer can leave their info for the tech support team if they need more help.
üë§ Human Touchpoint ‚Äî Deduplication

Repeat Questions ‚Üí Human Follow-Up

If the same person submits the same question twice in a day, the AI doesn't re-answer ‚Äî it says a human will follow up.
üìã Conversation Flows
One active conversation flow remains from the pre-generative AI era. It serves as the messaging fallback human touchpoint.
üìã Active Conversation Flow

"Click here to provide your contact information"

Mapped to the "request contact" intent. When the AI can't answer a messaging customer's question, this flow presents a button allowing them to leave their name, work email, and organization name for human follow-up. This is the primary escalation path from messaging to a human agent.
